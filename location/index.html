Norfolk

Located at the Hampton Roads Executive Airport

  • 10,000 square foot hangar facility
  • 5400-ft runway
  • 20 minute Uber ride from Norfolk International Airport
  • Major hotels, restaurants, and rental cars minutes from our facility in nearby Chesapeake

Chesapeake is a suburb of Norfolk and has a multitude of hospitality options in the Greenbriar area, as well as ample shopping and dining. Norfolk also provides significant opportunities for entertainment on the waterfront with rich historic neighborhoods and the Naval museum, festivals, breweries, harbor cruises, and fine dining & shopping. Plenty to keep your spouse entertained while you fly!

Atlanta

Located at the Newnan Coweta County Airport

  • 10,000 square foot hangar facility
  • 5600-ft runway
  • 25 minute Uber ride from Atlanta's International Airport
  • Major hotels, restaurants, and rental cars minutes from our facility

Newnan has a rich history and a thriving downtown square full of restaurants, bars, and retail shops. The court square andsurrounding several blocks of homes predate the Civil War during which Newnan served as a hospital town, and many of the buildingson the square date back to that period. The courthouse itself was built in 1901 on the site of the original hospital and has beenfeatured in numerous movies and tv shows. Plenty to keep your spouse entertained while you fly!
